Frequently Asked Questions

1. How much weight can I put in the dumpster?

Each container comes with a specific weight limit, typically measured in tons. If you exceed this limit, you may be charged an overage fee per ton. When you book your roll off dumpster rental in Lemont, IL, we will help you estimate the weight based on your debris type.

2. What happens if it rains on my open dumpster?

Rain can saturate debris, making absorbent materials like carpet or wood much heavier. This extra weight can lead to overage fees. We recommend covering your roll off dumpster in Lemont, IL with a tarp during storms to keep water weight to a minimum.

3. Can I put concrete in a standard dumpster?

Heavy materials like concrete, asphalt, or dirt cannot go into standard household trash bins because they exceed weight limits quickly. You must order a specific concrete dumpster rental near Lemont, IL designed to handle dense aggregates safely.

4. Is there a door on the dumpster?

Yes, almost all of our roll off units feature a swinging door at the rear. This allows you to walk heavy or bulky items directly into the waste container rental around Lemont, IL rather than lifting them over the side walls.

5. What items are prohibited?

For environmental safety, you cannot dispose of hazardous fluids, batteries, tires, asbestos, paints, or appliances containing Freon in a trash dumpster rental close to Lemont, IL. These items require specialized disposal methods.

6. Can I rent a dumpster for just one day?

While our standard rental periods are longer, we can accommodate shorter needs. If you fill the bin quickly, you can call for an early pickup, though the base price for the roll off rental near Lemont, IL typically remains the same.

Lemont, IL Insights: Population,
Zip Codes, Influence, and Service Areas

Lemont is a village located in Cook, DuPage, and Will counties in the U.S. state of Illinois, and is a south-west suburb of Chicago. The population was 17,629 as of the 2020 census. The village is situated on a hillside along the south banks of the Des Plaines River. It overlooks Waterfall Glen's Midwestern Bluff Savanna on the opposite side. Lemont is home to Argonne National Laboratory and other heavy industrial sites, and has a substantial European immigrant population.
