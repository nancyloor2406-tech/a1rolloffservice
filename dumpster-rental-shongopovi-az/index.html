Rain can saturate debris, making absorbent materials like carpet or wood much heavier. This extra weight can lead to overage fees. We recommend covering your roll off dumpster in Shongopovi, AZ with a tarp during storms to keep water weight to a minimum.

Heavy materials like concrete, asphalt, or dirt cannot go into standard household trash bins because they exceed weight limits quickly. You must order a specific concrete dumpster rental near Shongopovi, AZ designed to handle dense aggregates safely.
