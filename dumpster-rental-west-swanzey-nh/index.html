Frequently Asked Questions

1. How much weight can I put in the dumpster?

Each container comes with a specific weight limit, typically measured in tons. If you exceed this limit, you may be charged an overage fee per ton. When you book your roll off dumpster rental in West Swanzey, NH, we will help you estimate the weight based on your debris type.

2. What happens if it rains on my open dumpster?

Rain can saturate debris, making absorbent materials like carpet or wood much heavier. This extra weight can lead to overage fees. We recommend covering your roll off dumpster in West Swanzey, NH with a tarp during storms to keep water weight to a minimum.

3. Can I put concrete in a standard dumpster?

Heavy materials like concrete, asphalt, or dirt cannot go into standard household trash bins because they exceed weight limits quickly. You must order a specific concrete dumpster rental near West Swanzey, NH designed to handle dense aggregates safely.

Yes, almost all of our roll off units feature a swinging door at the rear. This allows you to walk heavy or bulky items directly into the waste container rental around West Swanzey, NH rather than lifting them over the side walls.

5. What items are prohibited?

For environmental safety, you cannot dispose of hazardous fluids, batteries, tires, asbestos, paints, or appliances containing Freon in a trash dumpster rental close to West Swanzey, NH. These items require specialized disposal methods.

6. Can I rent a dumpster for just one day?
